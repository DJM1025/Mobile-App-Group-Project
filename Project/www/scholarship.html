Scholarships

Application

The Slippery Rock Computer Science Department encourages all students to apply for scholarships Not only do scholarships help fund your education, they are a great resume builder for you to present to possible employers. Please feel free to visit this page for scholarship updates or contact the Computer Science Department at anytime if you have any questions. We are working hard to provide our dedicated students a chance to help them in funding their education.
